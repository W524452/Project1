Lunch at WAES is a not-for-profit organisation.

It exists to provide information about lunchtime options for attendees at WAES campus locations.

Lunch at WAES welcomes contributions from WAES attendees.

Please use the contact form if you wish to inform us of a new lunchtime option at any WAES campus.

Lunch at WAES is not affiliated with WAES, and no inference is to be drawn by its reference to WAES.
